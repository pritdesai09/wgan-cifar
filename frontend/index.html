How It Works

01

The Dataset

CIFAR-10 contains 60,000 32×32 colour images across 10 classes: planes, cars, birds, cats, deer, dogs, frogs, horses, ships and trucks.

02

WGAN-GP

Wasserstein GAN with Gradient Penalty replaces the standard GAN discriminator with a Critic trained using Earth Mover's Distance — giving more stable gradients and avoiding mode collapse.

03

Architecture

The Generator maps a 128-dim noise vector through four transposed convolution layers up to a 32×32 RGB image. The Critic mirrors this with strided convolutions and Instance Normalisation.

04

Training

100 epochs on Google Colab T4 GPU. Critic updates 5× per Generator update. λ=10 gradient penalty. Adam optimiser with β₁=0, β₂=0.9.
